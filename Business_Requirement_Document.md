# Business Requirement Document (BRD)
## Credit Risk Assessment & Model Validation

---

## 1. Background
Financial institutions face increasing risk from loan defaults due to inconsistent and manual credit evaluation processes. A data-driven credit risk assessment system is required to improve decision-making and reduce default rates.

---

## 2. Business Objective
To develop a statistical credit risk assessment solution that predicts the probability of loan default using historical customer and loan data.

---

## 3. Problem Statement
The current credit evaluation process lacks consistency and does not leverage historical data effectively, leading to higher default rates and inefficient loan approval decisions.

---

## 4. Scope of Work
- Analyze historical loan data
- Identify key risk drivers
- Build a statistical model to predict default probability
- Validate model performance using industry-standard metrics
- Provide interpretable outputs for business users

---

## 5. In-Scope
- Data preprocessing and analysis
- Model development (Logistic Regression)
- Model validation and performance assessment
- Documentation of business and technical details

---

## 6. Out-of-Scope
- Real-time deployment
- Integration with production banking systems
- Regulatory reporting automation

---

## 7. Success Criteria
- Model ROC-AUC greater than 0.75
- Stable and interpretable risk predictions
- Clear documentation supporting business decisions

---

## 8. Stakeholders
- Risk Management Team
- Credit Analysts
- Business Decision Makers
